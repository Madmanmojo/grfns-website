## Boot sequence complete.

If you're reading this, the site is up. That alone is worth a post.

`grfns.com` has existed as a domain for a while, sitting idle on shared hosting, doing nothing particularly useful. That changes now.

## What this place is

This is my corner of the internet. Part portfolio, part blog, part lab notebook. I'm going to write about the things I actually work on:

- Self-hosted infrastructure and homelabs
- Apple ecosystem and enterprise IT
- Automation, scripting, and tooling
- Whatever I'm building or breaking at any given moment

No SEO strategy. No content calendar. Just things worth writing down.

## What's running under the hood

The stack, since people always ask:

- **Next.js 16** on Vercel
- **Cloudflare** for DNS, CDN, and Zero Trust access
- **Nextcloud** on a RapidSeedbox VPS at `cloud.grfns.com`
- **Jellyfin** and a whole media automation stack running on Jarvis — my M4 Mac Mini
- **WireGuard** tying it all together

The whole thing came together in a single afternoon. That felt good.

## Where it's going

The about page has the career history. The blog will have the rest. At some point there'll be a portfolio section and maybe a merch store — I've been thinking about making some art, and 8-bit neon cyberpunk t-shirts feel like the right direction.
